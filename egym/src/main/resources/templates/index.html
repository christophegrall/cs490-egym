Weightlifting

Strength Muscle Endurance

Weightlifting, at it's core, is muscle strengthening and endurance. There are many different types of routines and exercises one can preform in order to acheive this. Most individuals set daily schedules in order to properly rotate muscle groups during the week. Some basic routes are as follows.



Routine One

Monday

  • Chest
    • Butterflys - 3 sets of 6 reps
    Barbell Bench Press - 3 sets of 6 reps
    Incline Dumbell Press - 3 sets of 6 reps
    Incline Bench Cable Fly - 3 sets of 6 reps
  • Biceps
  • Forearms

Tuesday

  • Chest
  • Biceps
  • Forearms
